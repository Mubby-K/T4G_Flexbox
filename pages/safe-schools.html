Special Initiative

Safe Schools, Safe Girls

Project Overview

The Safe Schools, Safe Girls initiative focuses heavily on driving academic equality by removing infrastructure-based barriers. Using precise data compilation, community partnerships, and rigorous school audits, we construct customized private sanitation layouts and deliver menstrual hygiene resources directly to rural academic environments.

Key Objectives & Actions

  • Comprehensive audits of local school sanitation infrastructures.
  • Installation of eco-conscious, private wash facilities tailored for privacy and comfort.
  • Hosting recurring health, safety, and hygiene workshops led by regional advisors.
  • Compiling performance data metrics to scale resources accurately into new regions.

How You Can Support

Every project requires funding for physical building materials, educational supplies, and regional coordination teams.
